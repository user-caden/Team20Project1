Our Management Team

At Khaana Bar & Grill, our success is driven by a passionate team of hospitality professionals dedicated to delivering exceptional dining experiences. Each member brings unique expertise and a shared commitment to quality, service, and community.

Executive Leadership

Rajesh Patel - Founder & CEO
With over 20 years in the restaurant industry, Rajesh founded Khaana with a vision to blend traditional flavors with modern bar and grill cuisine. His background in culinary arts and business management has shaped our restaurant's unique identity and commitment to excellence.

Maria Gonzales - Chief Operating Officer
Maria oversees daily operations across all locations, ensuring every guest receives outstanding service. Her expertise in operational efficiency and staff development has been instrumental in maintaining our high standards.

Kitchen Leadership

Chef Andre Williams - Executive Chef
A culinary artist with a passion for fusion cuisine, Chef Andre crafts our seasonal menus and trains our kitchen staff. His innovative approach to bar and grill classics has earned Khaana numerous local awards.

Our Values

The management team at Khaana operates on core principles:

  1. Guest-First Mentality - Every decision prioritizes customer satisfaction
  2. Team Development - Investing in our staff's growth and success
  3. Community Engagement - Supporting local suppliers and charitable causes
